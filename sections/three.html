what's your lifestyle ?

While discovering new healthy snacks is a lot of fun, we know “healthy” doesn’t mean the same for everyone . That’s why we’ve put our heads together and created 5 awesome lifestyle categories together with nutrition experts.

Each snack is labelled with a lifestyle category sticker and makes it easy to quickly choose the perfect snack for any situation .

These snacks are low-carb and have only little sugar . They're a source of valuable vitamins & minerals . Whenever you're up for a snack but want to keep extra calories on a minimum, "Slim & Trim" snacks will be your best friend .

Slim & Trim

Fit & Active

These snacks have a lot of proteins and may also have a decent level of sugar . Take a “Fit & Active” snack before, while or after physical workout to get most out of it !

Push & Perform

These snacks contain more sugar but often bring along fibres & valuable vitamins . "Push & Perform" snacks are the perfect energy booster whenever you feel low in energy of concentration . Be aware though of eating them all day long, extra sugar means extra calories .

Rewarding Moment

These snacks may contain less valuable nutrients . Still they are of high quality and obviously great in taste : So grab one when you feel like you just so much deserved that one right now !
